Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my order to be processed and shipped?

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days from the date of purchase. Once your order is processed, it will be shipped out within the same or the following business day.

What is your return policy?

Our return policy is designed to ensure customer satisfaction and provide you with peace of mind when shopping with us. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may return the item(s) within 30 days of the purchase date. The item(s) must be in new and unused condition, with all original tags and packaging intact. We cannot accept returns on items that have been worn, altered, or damaged after receipt.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment options to make your shopping experience as convenient as possible. This includes all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and gift cards. All of our payment options are secure and PCI-compliant to protect your personal and financial information. If you have any questions or concerns about our payment options, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team for assistance.

Can I pre-order sneakers that are not yet released?

Yes, we offer pre-orders on select upcoming releases. Please refer to the product page for details and estimated release dates.

How do I know if the sneakers will fit me?

We recommend referring to the sizing chart on the product page or using our size guide to determine the best size for you. If you're still unsure, feel free to contact our customer service team for assistance.

Can I track my order?

Yes, we will provide a tracking number once your order has shipped. You can track your order on the shipping carrier's website using this number.
